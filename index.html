Hi, I'm Calie!

Your personal, intelligent scheduling assistant

At the palm of your hands, Calie will keep track of your scheduling habits and ensure you commit to your to-do lists.
Features:
  • Calie chats with you regularly to understand your priorities (which you might not even know!) through Microsoft's Azure Machine Learning integration.
  • Overwhelming to-do list, where over 50% never gets done? Not to worry, Calie's got you covered! She will propose schedules that best fit your priorities.
  • What more can she do? With a collaboration with companies such as Groupon and Eventbrite, she will suggest resources to commit to your checklist!
